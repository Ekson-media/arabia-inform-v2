Our Story

From Vision to a Living Arabic Archive

In 1997, Arabia Inform began with a singular conviction: that the Arabic-speaking world's knowledge — its news, scholarship, broadcast, and intellectual heritage — must be captured, preserved, and made accessible. Not partially. Not selectively. Completely.

Operating from two dedicated processing centers running 24/7, Arabia Inform has never missed a single day of coverage in over 29 years. What began as a media clipping service has become the world's most comprehensive repository of Arabic knowledge — spanning 3+ billion online articles, 280+ million print clips, 120+ million broadcast hours, and 1.7 million academic papers.

Today, Arabia Inform serves governments, universities, research institutions, corporations, and AI companies across the globe — providing the data, intelligence, and historical context that powers decisions, shapes policy, and advances Arabic language technology.

Mission

Preserving the Arabic Memory. Powering Decisions.
